Description

General

Kwik EFIS is a Glass Cockpit application designed to work on most Android devices equipped with a GPS, gyroscope, accelerometer and a CPU with reasonable performance.

Initial test flights with Kwik EFIS has been encouraging. Results are better on the Nexus 7 tablet then the Galaxy S2 phone but that is to be expected since the sensors on the various Android devices vary greatly in quality and performance.

Fusing for GPS inputs now take a greater precedence over the gyros. This helps with stabilizing the roll filter a little better.

The slip/roll coupling uses a low pass filter on all the sensors. This helps with some of the inferior gyroscrope and accelerometer sensors on some of the cheaper Android devices.

Flight Director

Kwik EFIS has a fully functional flight director built in. It uses the standard V-Bar symbology common to modern flight directors. The target waypoint and altitude is set on-screen by means of the spinner controls on the right top and bottom of the screen.

Demo Mode

There is also a "Demo Mode" available in the application. It is fairly rudimentary and basically works like a crude flight simulator. The heading and altitude can be changed by pitching and banking the device. The speed runs up and down automatically based on the pitch.
